## FCAJ Community Day

**Location:** Bitexco Financial Tower, 26th Floor, Ho Chi Minh City
---

### Overview

**FCAJ Community Day** is a community event organized by First Cloud Journey, bringing together experts in Cloud, DevOps, and Generative AI. The event provides an opportunity for interns to interact directly with experienced speakers, learn about company culture, career opportunities, and the latest technology trends on AWS.

---

### Event Agenda

| Time | Content |
|-----------|---------|
| 8:30 – 9:00 AM | Check-in at the 26th Floor |
| 9:00 – 9:45 AM | **Building Modern Platform Engineering & Career Pathways** — Introduction to company culture and internship opportunities; an overview of Platform Engineering within the Cloud and DevOps ecosystem; Q&A with experts |
| 9:45 – 10:15 AM | **GenAIOps Essential — DevOps for Generative AI Applications** — DevOps Fundamentals on AWS; real-world GenAIOps examples featuring Bedrock AgentCore Observability, EKS, and Langfuse |
| 10:15 – 10:45 AM | **Shipping Code in the Agentic Era** — Challenges, tools, and a deep dive into programming in the AI Agent era; Productivity Playbook and live Demo |
| 10:45 – 11:00 AM | Tea Break |
| 11:00 – 11:30 AM | **Production-Grade Multimodal GenAI on AWS** — The new AI Application Stack; Multimodal Search with Nova Embeddings; GraphRAG for Enterprise Knowledge; Multi-Agent Workflows; Safe & Observable GenAI |
| 11:30 – 12:00 PM | **From Edge To Origin: CloudFront as Your Foundation** — Amazon CloudFront for all workloads; optimizing costs, security, reliability, and performance |

---

### Key Takeaways

By attending this event, I had the opportunity to directly explore the most highly anticipated topics in the industry today:

- **Platform Engineering & Career Pathways:** Gained a clearer understanding of career paths in the Cloud domain, specifically the intersection of Platform Engineering and DevOps in the AWS environment. The open Q&A session allowed me to ask experts directly about my post-internship career directions.

- **GenAIOps Essential:** Learned how to apply DevOps practices to the Generative AI application development lifecycle — a topic highly relevant to the SmartHire AI project I am building, especially regarding the Amazon Bedrock integration.

- **Agentic Era & AI Coding Tools:** Realized the significant shift in programming trends with the emergence of AI Agents — allowing me to adjust my learning and working approach accordingly.

- **Production-Grade Multimodal GenAI:** Explored how to build large-scale, safe, and observable multimodal AI systems — foundational knowledge for my future AI projects.
